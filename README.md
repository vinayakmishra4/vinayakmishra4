## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🌾 [Sugarcane Production Data Analysis](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Sugarcane-Production)

A data cleaning and EDA project exploring **global sugarcane production trends** across 100+ countries.

**Key Insights:**
- Identified the top 10 sugarcane-producing nations and their output share over 20+ years
- Detected data quality issues including missing yields and inconsistent country name formats
- Revealed regional production shifts between South Asia, Latin America, and Africa over time
- Prepared a clean, analysis-ready dataset as a foundation for yield prediction modeling

**Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn

[→ View Project](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Sugarcane-Production)

---

### ❤️ [Heart Disease Data Analysis](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Heart-Disease-Analysis)

An exploratory data analysis project uncovering **clinical and demographic risk factors** associated with heart disease.

**Key Insights:**
- Found that chest pain type and maximum heart rate are among the strongest predictors of disease presence
- Identified that male patients in the 50–65 age range had the highest disease prevalence in the dataset
- Visualized correlations between features like cholesterol, resting blood pressure, and diagnosis outcome
- Cleaned and structured the dataset to serve as a clean input for future classification models

**Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn

[→ View Project](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Heart-Disease-Analysis)

---

### 🛍️ [Black Friday Sales Analysis](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Black-Firday-Sales-Analysis)

A comprehensive EDA on retail transaction data to uncover **customer purchasing behavior and revenue drivers**.

**Key Insights:**
- The **26–35 age group** drove the highest purchase volume, accounting for the largest revenue segment
- **Male customers** contributed significantly more to total sales than female customers across all categories
- **City Category B** outperformed A and C, suggesting stronger retail penetration in mid-tier cities
- Top product categories (001, 005, 008) were responsible for a disproportionate share of total revenue
- Identified customer segments for targeted marketing and prepared the dataset for machine learning modeling

**Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn

[→ View Project](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Black-Firday-Sales-Analysis)

---

### 🌍 [Global GDP Analysis](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Gross-Domestic-Product-Analysis)

A data analysis project exploring **global economic trends and growth patterns** across 200+ countries using historical GDP data.

**Key Insights:**
- Mapped GDP growth trajectories for G20 nations over three decades, revealing diverging economic paths
- Identified emerging economies in Southeast Asia and Africa with consistent above-average growth rates
- Highlighted sharp GDP contractions during 2008–09 and 2020, with varying recovery speeds by region
- Standardized data formats across 200+ countries and handled missing values for reliable cross-country comparison

**Tools:** Python · Pandas · NumPy · Matplotlib · Seaborn

[→ View Project](https://github.com/vinayakmishra4/Project-Gross-Domestic-Product-Analysis)
